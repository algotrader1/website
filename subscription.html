2. Type of Purchase — One-Time, Non-Recurring

All plans offered through our quiz are one-time purchases. This means:

  • You are charged once at the time of purchase.
  • Your plan provides access for the exact duration of the plan you selected.
  • You will not be automatically re-billed at the end of the access period.
  • You will not be enrolled in any recurring subscription as part of your quiz purchase.

If you wish to continue using Onera beyond the end of your plan, you may choose to purchase a new plan or subscribe through the Onera iOS app, which is billed separately by Apple.

3. Processing & Access

Payments are processed securely by Stripe, Inc., our payment service provider. We never store your full card details on our servers. Once payment is confirmed, you will receive:

  • An email confirmation with your receipt and order number.
  • A secure magic-link to activate your Onera account and open the iOS app.
  • Access to your personalized dream plan and all included features for the duration of your plan.

4. iOS In-App Subscription (Separate From Quiz Purchase)

The Onera iOS app also offers its own auto-renewing subscriptions managed entirely by Apple through the App Store. These subscriptions are separate from the one-time quiz purchase and follow Apple's standard subscription rules:

  • Payment is charged to your Apple ID account upon confirmation.
  • The subscription automatically renews unless auto-renew is turned off at least 24 hours before the end of the current period.
  • Your Apple ID account will be charged for renewal within 24 hours before the end of the current period.
  • You may manage and cancel your iOS subscriptions in your Apple ID account settings at any time.
  • Any unused portion of a free trial, if offered, will be forfeited when you purchase an auto-renewing subscription.

For more details, please refer to Apple's subscription management documentation.

5. Refund Policy

30-day money-back guarantee. If for any reason you are not satisfied with your one-time quiz purchase, you may request a full refund within 30 days of the original purchase date by emailing contact@dreaminsight.app with your order number or the email address used at checkout.

We typically process refund requests within 5 business days. Once approved, refunds are returned to your original payment method and may take an additional 5–10 business days to appear depending on your bank.

Refunds for iOS in-app subscriptions are handled by Apple and cannot be processed directly by Onera. Please visit reportaproblem.apple.com to request an App Store refund.

6. Cancellation

Because quiz plans are one-time purchases with no recurring billing, there is nothing to "cancel" after you have paid — your access simply ends at the conclusion of the plan duration.

To cancel an iOS auto-renewing subscription, open Settings on your iPhone, tap your Apple ID, then Subscriptions, and turn off auto-renew for Onera.

7. Changes to Plans or Prices

We may update our plans, pricing, or promotional discounts at any time. Any changes will apply only to purchases made after the change; purchases already completed will retain the price and plan you agreed to at checkout.

8. Contact

For any question about this Subscription Policy, billing, refunds, or account access, please contact contact@dreaminsight.app. We aim to reply within 1–2 business days.
