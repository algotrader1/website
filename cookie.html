Cookie Policy

Last updated: April 12, 2026

This Cookie Policy explains how Onera ("we", "us", "our") uses cookies and similar tracking technologies on our website dreaminsight.app and our dream assessment quiz. By continuing to use our services, you consent to the use of cookies as described below.

1. What Are Cookies

Cookies are small text files that are stored on your device when you visit a website. They allow the website to recognize your device and remember information about your visit, such as preferences, quiz progress, and authentication status.

We also use similar technologies such as local storage, session storage, and tracking pixels (for example the Meta Pixel) to provide, analyze, and improve our services.

2. Types of Cookies We Use

Essential Cookies

These cookies are strictly necessary for the website and quiz to function. They enable core functionality such as saving quiz progress between steps, maintaining your session, and processing secure payments via Stripe. You cannot opt out of essential cookies.

Analytics & Performance Cookies

These cookies help us understand how visitors interact with the quiz and the site. We use them to measure conversion rates, identify technical issues, and improve the user experience. All analytics data is aggregated and anonymized.

Advertising & Marketing Cookies

We use advertising cookies and tracking pixels (including the Meta Pixel from Meta Platforms, Inc.) to measure the performance of our advertising campaigns, to show you relevant ads on Facebook, Instagram and other platforms, and to build audiences for future campaigns. These cookies may be set by us or by third-party advertising partners.

3. Third Parties Who May Set Cookies

Provider Purpose
Meta Platforms (Facebook Pixel) Conversion tracking, ad targeting, audience building
Stripe Secure payment processing, fraud prevention
Supabase Authentication, database session management
Google Fonts Delivering web fonts (no tracking)

4. How Long Cookies Last

  • Session cookies: deleted automatically when you close your browser.
  • Persistent cookies: remain on your device for a set period (typically 30 days to 2 years) or until you delete them manually.
  • Quiz progress: stored in session storage and expires after 30 minutes of inactivity.

5. Your Choices

You can control and/or delete cookies at any time. Most browsers allow you to refuse or delete cookies through their settings. Please note that disabling essential cookies may prevent the quiz and the checkout from functioning correctly.

To opt out of personalized advertising on Meta platforms, visit your Meta ad preferences. For general information on opting out of interest-based advertising, visit the Digital Advertising Alliance opt-out page or the European Interactive Digital Advertising Alliance.

6. Do Not Track

Some browsers offer a "Do Not Track" (DNT) signal. Because there is no industry standard for how to respond to DNT signals, our website does not currently respond to them. We continue to monitor developments on this topic.

7. Updates to This Policy

We may update this Cookie Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices, our services, or applicable law. The "Last updated" date at the top of this page indicates when the policy was last revised. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.

8. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Cookie Policy or our use of cookies, please contact us at contact@dreaminsight.app.
