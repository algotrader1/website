4. Meta Pixel & Conversions API

We use Meta Pixel (browser-side) and Conversions API (server-side) to measure the effectiveness of our advertising campaigns on Facebook and Instagram. This includes:

  • Tracking events such as PageView, quiz completion (Lead), and purchase (Purchase)
  • Sending your hashed email address to Meta for conversion matching (Meta cannot read your actual email)
  • Using _fbc and _fbp cookies for attribution

You can opt out of Meta tracking by:

  • Adjusting your Facebook Ad Preferences
  • Using browser privacy tools or ad blockers
  • Contacting us to request removal from tracking (see Section 9)

5. Data Retention

  • Active accounts: We retain your data for as long as your account is active and you have an active subscription
  • After cancellation: We retain your data for 30 days after account deletion to allow for reactivation, then permanently delete it
  • Payment records: Transaction records may be retained for up to 7 years as required by tax and financial regulations
  • Anonymized data: Aggregated, anonymized analytics data may be retained indefinitely as it cannot be linked back to you

6. Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your personal information:

  • Encryption in transit: All data transmitted between your device and our servers uses TLS/SSL encryption (HTTPS)
  • Encryption at rest: Database contents are encrypted at rest via Supabase's infrastructure
  • PCI compliance: Payment processing is handled entirely by Stripe (PCI-DSS Level 1 certified) — we never handle raw card data
  • Access controls: Internal access to user data is restricted to authorized personnel only

No system is 100% secure. While we strive to protect your data, we cannot guarantee absolute security.
