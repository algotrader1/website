Frequently Asked Questions

Before reaching out, check our FAQ — your answer might be just a click away.

What is Onera?+
Onera is a comprehensive dream work program available on iOS. It provides dream analysis based on Jungian psychology, body-emotion mapping, guided somatic exercises, and tools to support your journey to self-discovery and healing.
I never remember my dreams. Can I still use it?+
Yes. Dream Recall Mastery is built into Onera. Most users go from "I never dream" to 4-5 dreams per week within 10 days. Voice recording the moment you wake up makes it effortless.
What's somatic release?+
Simple guided exercises — breathing, gentle movement, body awareness. 15 minutes, no experience needed. Based on Peter Levine's research. Your body already knows how to do this.
Can it replace my therapist?+
Not at all — Onera complements therapy beautifully. It adds the physical release step that talk alone can't do. Many users say it deepens and accelerates their existing practice.
What's the pricing?+
We offer a flexible annual plan at $69.99/year ($5.83/month). It includes a 7-day free trial and our risk-free guarantee lets you cancel anytime without paying the full price.
Is my dream data private?+
Absolutely. ONERA uses end-to-end encryption — your dreams are encrypted on your device before being stored. No one, not even us, can read your journal entries.
Is ONERA available on Android?+
ONERA is currently available on iOS (iPhone and iPad). An Android version is planned — join our email list to be notified when it launches.
How is ONERA different from ChatGPT for dream analysis?+
ChatGPT gives generic interpretations. ONERA combines Jungian psychology with somatic release exercises — it doesn't just tell you what your dream means, it helps your body process the emotions your dreams surface.
