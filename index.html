It's non-linear.
It's raw.
It's the unfair advantage every genius in history secretly used.

And you? You keep deleting it with your morning piss.

Wake up.
Write your dream.
Decode it.

7 days later → fire in your chest.
The version of you that never settled.
The version of you that people can't ignore.
